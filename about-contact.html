About Hoshiya

Hoshiya is a design-first shop blending manga-inspired visuals with curated lifestyle products. We focus on accessible, fast, and playful shopping experiences.

Our Story

Founded by creators and fans of graphic storytelling, Hoshiya aims to bring bold illustration and careful craftsmanship to small-batch apparel, collectibles, and art objects.
