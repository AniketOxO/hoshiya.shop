    Damaged Packages

    If your package arrives damaged:

    1. Do not accept the delivery if the outer package is severely damaged
    2. Take photos of the damaged package
    3. Contact us within 48 hours at ezyarohaclo@gmail.com
    4. We'll arrange for a replacement or refund immediately

    Lost Packages

    If your tracking shows "delivered" but you haven't received your package:

    1. Check with neighbors, building security, or household members
    2. Look around your delivery location (porch, garage, side door)
    3. Wait 24 hours (sometimes packages are marked delivered before actual delivery)
    4. Contact us if still not received - we'll file a claim with the courier

    Delayed Deliveries

    Delays may occur due to:

    • Weather conditions or natural disasters
    • Public holidays and festivals
    • Customs clearance (for international orders)
    • Incorrect or incomplete address
    • Remote delivery locations

    We'll notify you of any significant delays. Contact us if your order is delayed beyond the estimated delivery date.
